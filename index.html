The future of microelectronics is going to be more multi-functional and heterogeneous than ever impacting everyday life functions in the society as we approach the AI era. At the EESoC Lab in SUSTech, our mission is to power the AI era with innovative circuit and system design techniques and methodologies to deliver extreme miniaturized smart sensing platforms for healthcare, Internet-of-Things, and infrastructure instrumentation. We do cross-disciplinary research to link the physical world with cloud using fully-customized microsystems for better energy efficiency, performance, robustness, and longer lifetime. Our research area includes:

  • Ultra-low power low voltage sensor-to-digital front-end with a focus on investigating voltage/time domain low noise and offset sensor interfaces and analog-to digital converters with highly digital implementations for energy harvesting applications.
  • Energy efficient medical/IoT radio with an emphasis on compact digital-intensive architectures with increased spectrum efficiency and robust operation under PVT variation.
    • Smart sensor for personalized healthcare with a focus on developing energy efficient SRAM-based in-memory computing circuits and architectures to provide real-time diagnosis and feedback:
  • Please learn more details about our on-going efforts by visiting the Research page.
